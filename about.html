A little bit about me...

Hey, I'm Khia and I'm currently participating in the She Codes Plus Program!

I'm GIS Analyst (Geographic Information Systems) intern at Winyama, a Digital Solutions Organisation empowering Indigenous mob in the tech space. I'm reasonably new to the job, and my role includes visualising data for rangers who work out on Country. As someone who is new to the role and an Intern, I'm learning the ropes of mapping software, Earth Observations, data capturing technology and Native Title.

Its very exciting to be learning in the tech space and to be encouraged by other women who are on their own learning discoveries.

At She Codes, We are learning how to code. The languages we are learning include:

Python

HTML CSS & Javascript

Django

& React!
